🎪 CLOWN AROUND 🎪

Everyone will work together to create a unique clown with different outcomes. Each person will add something to the drawing, but it will be done under a time limit. You can add almost anything to the clown, like different outfits, makeup, hairstyles, etc. and by the end you should have a unique clown that fits into your circus. This can be done multiple times to create different types of clowns.

Tools: timer, writing utensils (markers, pencils, crayons, colored pencils), paper

Participants: 3–5 people

Time: 10 minutes

Instructions

To start, each participant will be given a color of their choice, and the first person will draw the base of the clown for 2 minutes. They can choose to only draw a head, or create a full body clown. The person who draws the base of the clown will be decided by a game of rock paper scissors. You may add onto another person's drawing, or color something in.

If the base of the clown is drawn, then the paper will be passed around to the next person who will add onto the paper.

If thirty seconds passes, then the next person adds something onto the clown in their own color.

If five minutes have passed, then there must be a nose, mouth, and eyes on the clown.

If there are no facial features within the first five minutes, then the timer will shorten to twenty seconds each. If the timer hits 10 minutes, then the drawing is finished.
